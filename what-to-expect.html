What to Expect When You Join

Benefits of Joining

Becoming a Mike Chandler Fan Member means being part of an exclusive community that celebrates speed, passion, and connection. Here's what each membership level offers:

Standard Membership – $150

  • Access to monthly newsletters and race updates
  • Early bird ticket access to select events
  • Member welcome packet with sticker & lanyard
  • 10% off official merchandise

VIP Membership – $200

  • Everything in Standard Membership
  • VIP pit access (select events)
  • Signed photo of Mike Chandler
  • Exclusive members-only race day meetups
  • 15% off official merchandise

All-Access Membership – $400

  • Everything in VIP Membership
  • 1 Free Meet & Greet Pass
  • Access to exclusive behind-the-scenes content
  • Mike Chandler T-shirt
  • Personalized birthday shout-out from Mike

After You Apply

Once you've selected and applied for your membership level, here's what happens:

  • You will receive a confirmation email with details about your membership.
  • Your welcome packet (if applicable) will be mailed to your provided address.
  • You'll gain immediate access to online perks like newsletters and forums.
  • Event updates and merchandise discounts will be activated within 48 hours.
  • For All-Access members, further instructions regarding the Meet & Greet and birthday shout-out will be shared via email.
